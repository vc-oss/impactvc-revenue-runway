# Partner Types

Defines the company-level relationship between organizations.

## Referral Partner
Provides warm introductions without tracked links.

## Affiliate
Refers via trackable links with attribution.

## Channel Partner
Actively sells or resells offerings as part of their own motion.

## Strategic Partner
Collaborates on long-term positioning and market education.

## Co-Marketing Partner
Collaborates on specific campaigns or initiatives.

## Investor
Provides capital without operational responsibility.

## Vendor
Sells tools or services to us.

## Community Partner
Provides access to an audience or community.

## Other
Catch-all for undefined or experimental relationships.
