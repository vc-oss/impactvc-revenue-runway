# Contact Types

Defines who a person is to the business.  
This does not imply lifecycle, intent, or engagement status.

## Lead
A person who has expressed interest but has not yet transacted.

## Customer
A person associated with a company that has purchased.

## Partner
A person representing a partner company.

## Partner Customer
A person who is both a partner contact and a customer.

## Contractor
A non-employee providing services (sales, fractional, delivery).

## Vendor
A person representing a company that sells tools or services to us.

## Media / Press
A person representing media or publications.

## Advisor
A person providing strategic guidance without execution responsibility.

## Internal
Employees or internal team members.

## Other
Catch-all for edge cases.
