# MQL Pipeline

## Purpose
Qualifies inbound and SDR-sourced accounts before promotion to SQL.

---

## Stages (in order)

1. New – Unqualified  
All inbound and SDR-sourced opportunities enter here.
No ICP decision has been made.

2. ICP Yes  
Account meets Ideal Customer Profile criteria.

3. Nurture Yes  
ICP-fit but not sales-ready.

4. ICP Qualification Meeting Set  
A qualifying conversation is scheduled.

5. ICP Qualified – Move to SQL Pipeline  
Exit stage. Automation moves opportunity to SQL.

6. ICP Not Qualified – Nurture / Disqualify  
Explicit non-ICP or poor-fit accounts.

---

## Entry Rules
- Default entry for inbound and SDR-created deals.

## Exit Rules
- Promotion to SQL occurs only from Stage 5.
