# SQL Pipeline

## Purpose
Tracks sales-qualified opportunities through the active sales process.

---

## Stages (in order)

1. Discovery  
Problem exploration and fit confirmation.

2. Proposal Meeting  
Solution and pricing presented.

3. Negotiate  
Commercial, legal, or procurement adjustments.

4. Pending Win Review 
Deal win pending review for automation.

5. Pending Loss Review  
Deal loss pending review for automation. 

4. Close Won  
Deal successfully closed.

5. Close Lost  
Deal exited.

---

## Entry Rules
- Entered when sales qualification criteria are met.
- May enter directly (outbound sales) or via MQL promotion.

## Velocity Anchor
- All sales velocity is measured from Deal Entered SQL Date.
